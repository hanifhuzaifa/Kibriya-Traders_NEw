About Us

Your Total Business Remedy
Located in the centre of Pakistan, Karachi is where we are based.

Since Covid 2020, we have been assisting people around the world to meet their needs more easily and expand their home-based businesses. It is difficult for us to give them the best products for their use or businesses, but we have worked hard to build the platform that enables global sourcing and work-from-home selling solutions. We are service providers; we help you with product procurement, product research, and shipping to the address of your choice.

Product Hunting & Sourcing

Finding the finest thing to market, sourcing it, and producing it Kibriya Trader manages and offers a full end-to-end manufacturing solution, handling everything from concept and design to sample and the production process.

Graphic Designing

We separate out from other providers because to our in-house graphic design team and expertise of your clientele. Our designers have a proven track record of producing finished products by adhering to product briefs and style manuals.

Shipping & Logistics:

Our logistics team streamlines the entire shipment procedure. We take care of all the boring administrative procedures and provide all the goods you require. Airfreight, air cargo, LCL, and FCL are among the services. Additionally, we offer a gate-to-gate service. (from the manufacturer to the location you choose)
